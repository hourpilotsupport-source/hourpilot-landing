Privacy Policy

HourPilot is a local-first browser extension for Toggl Track.


HourPilot does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.


Your Toggl API token and planning data are stored locally in your browser using Chrome storage and are never transmitted to external servers controlled by HourPilot.


HourPilot does not use analytics, tracking scripts, advertising networks, or third-party telemetry systems.


HourPilot connects directly to the Toggl API only to retrieve project and tracking information required for extension functionality.


HourPilot is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Toggl.
